About This Application

CKM Karoseri TikTok Content Tool is a private desktop application used exclusively by CKM Karoseri to automate video uploads to TikTok via the official TikTok Content Posting API. This tool helps manage and schedule karoseri car modification video content for our TikTok business account.

How It Works

The application authenticates with TikTok using OAuth 2.0, then allows the account owner to upload videos directly from local storage to TikTok. No data is shared with third parties. The tool is not distributed publicly and is used solely for internal content management.
